About Me

Hey, I'm Matthew. I live in London with my wife, my little brother Chris, and our lion called Yeboah. Most mornings I wake up late, sleep again, and still somehow make it to school before assembly ends - usually.

I'm not the loudest person in my class, but I pay attention. I like learning things that feel useful, especially when it comes to computers and design. On weekends you'll find me watching football highlights, helping my mum in the kitchen, or scrolling TikTok way longer than I should.

This page is just me - the real me. Not perfect, not trying too hard. Just a boy from Ghana figuring out who he wants to become.

Where I'm Headed

Right now I'm focused on passing my WASSCE and getting into a good university. I know the road is not easy - fees, pressure, everyone asking what you want to do with your life - but I believe hard work pays. My mum always says, "Slowly slowly, the bird builds its nest."
